Correspondence Zone

Leah Misemer | Georgia Institute of Technology
Posted 15 September 2018

Leah Misemer is a Marion L. Brittain Postdoctoral Fellow at Georgia Institute of Technology. Her research draws together various forms and genres of comics from around the world to consider how serial publication structure--online and in print--has allowed marginalized audiences to build communities. You can find her work in Composition Studies and Forum for World Literature Studies, as well as forthcoming in several edited collections.

Recommended citation:

Misemer, Leah. “Correspondence Zone.” Sequentials, vol. 1, no. 2, 2018.
